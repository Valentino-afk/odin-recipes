Cake Recipe

Description:

A delicious cake that one single slice restores 2 of hunger and 0.4 of saturation (have 7 slice), easy to do, does not need to be cooked, does not rot and you can feed pandas!.

Ingredients:

  • 3 milk buckets
  • 2 of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 of wheat

Steps:

  1. Open your crafting table.
  2. Put the 3 buckets in the first three slots (slot one, slot two and slot three).
  3. Put one of sugar in the fourth slot.
  4. Put the egg in the fifth spot.
  5. Put the last of sugar in the sixth spot.
  6. Put the 3 of wheat in the last three slots (slot seven, slot eighth and slot nine).
  7. Pick up the cake that appears in the rigth.
