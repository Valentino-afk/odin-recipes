Cookie Recipe

Description:

A delicious cookies that restore 2 of hunger and 0.4 of saturation, easy to do, does not need to be cooked, it takes very few ingredients and does not rot (this recipe makes 8 cookies!).

Ingredients:

  • 2 of wheat
  • 1 cocoa beans

Steps:

  1. Open your crafting table.
  2. Put one of wheat in the fourth slot.
  3. Put the cocoa beansin the fifth slot.
  4. Put the last wheat in the sixth slot.
  5. Pick up the 8 cookie that appears at the right.
